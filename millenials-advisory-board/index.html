Millenials Advisory Board

Millennials or the Millennial Generation, also known as Generation Y refers to people born in the 1980s and the 1990s, usually ending by 1995.

DMG Worldwide Consulting has established its Millennials Advisory Board to take the expertise that comes with experience and combine it with the knowledge of modern technology and current culture – the signature of today’s new workforce generation.

By embracing the intellectual curiosity of the millennial age group, DMG Consulting is providing a venue for younger professionals to experience business first hand, and to witness the ways in which their education translates into the real world.

In return, these millenials will offer their insight regarding prospective clients, new business deals, the latest products and services and potential partnerships. With the skills that these board members possess, they will be able to dissect and assess the possibilities and supply a perspective that only comes with their capabilities and age.
